TEEN TALKS - HIV/AIDS AWARENESS CAMPAIGN

Web Development - Desing - Branding

As final project on my last semester at college, my team and I were assigned to create an awareness campaign for the Regional HIV/AIDS Connection organization. This campaign was targeted towards teenagers from 15 to 18 years old, therefore we focused to display our campaign on social media and other digital platforms. For this project I produced and animated interactive videos for social media and YouTube.

Role: Developer, Designer & Motion Designer
