SPORTS DATA VISUALIZATION

Motion Design

Motion design project where I used animated HUD graphics to simulate real time data visualizations that analyze an athlete's movements and resulting forces captured in slow motion. By using shape layers, 3D layers and motion tracking techniques in Adobe After Effects I was able to demonstrate the benefits of diagnosing and visualizing athletic movement, forces of professional sports.
