ARBORA POINT OF PURCHASE DISPLAYS

Branding - Design

I was tasked to design and 3D model point of purchase displays for the Arbora campaign. I designed two displays for retail stores, a free-standing display and a counter display. First, I sketched some ideas on paper, then I used cinema 4D in order to create 3D models of the displays and finally I created mock-ups and video animations to show all angles of the display.

Role: Designer & Motion Designer
